VitalWear

NEONATAL MONITORING SUIT

VitalWear is an advanced wearable monitoring system designed specifically for neonatal intensive care. It continuously tracks 4 essential vital signs — Heart Rate, SpO2, Respiratory Rate, and ECG — providing real-time data to care teams around the clock.

Our Mission

To revolutionize neonatal care by delivering real-time, wearable monitoring technology that empowers healthcare professionals with actionable insights — reducing response time in critical moments and improving patient outcomes for the most vulnerable patients.

Our Vision

To become the global standard in neonatal vital monitoring, making NICU-grade care accessible in every hospital worldwide. We envision a future where no newborn goes unmonitored, regardless of location or resources.

Our Values

Excellence in engineering, compassion for patients, and relentless innovation drive every decision we make. We believe in transparent collaboration with healthcare teams, ethical use of patient data, and continuous improvement guided by clinical feedback.

How VitalWear Works

1. Wearable Suit

Ultra-soft biosensor suit placed on the newborn captures continuous biometric data.

2. Wireless Sync

Data transmitted securely via BLE/Wi-Fi to the hospital network in real time.

3. AI Analysis

AI engine analyzes patterns, detects anomalies, and generates intelligent clinical alerts.

4. Care Team Alert

Doctors and nurses receive instant notifications on this dashboard and via SMS/email.
